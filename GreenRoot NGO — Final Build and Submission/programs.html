Our Programs

Four ways GreenRoot shows up in a neighborhood, year-round and built to grow with it.

Program list

🌳

Tree Planting Drives

Half-day weekend plantings on streets and school grounds. We supply the saplings, tools, and a crew lead who knows the soil.

🧹

Neighborhood Clean-Up Days

Monthly clean-ups for parks, riverbanks, and vacant lots. Gloves, bags, and pickup are all arranged ahead of time.

📚

Sustainability Workshops

Free evening sessions on composting, cutting home energy use, and growing food in small spaces. No experience needed.

🥕

Community Garden Network

Shared garden plots across six sites, with tools, water access, and a season-long planting calendar.

How it works

  1. Find a program

    Pick whichever one fits your time and interests, near or far from home.

  2. Sign up

    Register online from the Get Involved page, or just show up to a public clean-up.

  3. Show up and dig in

    We'll have everything you need on site, the rest is just turning up.

Found a program you like?

Sign up in a couple of minutes and we'll take it from there.
