Frequently asked questions

Do I need experience to volunteer?

No experience needed for any of our programs. Crew leads walk you through everything on the day.

Can my company sponsor a tree-planting day?

Yes, we run group plantings for businesses and schools. Email us and we'll put together a date that works.

What should I bring to a clean-up day?

Closed-toe shoes and clothes you don't mind getting dirty. We provide gloves, bags, and tools.

Is there a minimum age to volunteer?

Most programs welcome ages 12 and up with a guardian. Workshops are open to all ages.
