Who we are

GreenRoot started in 2015 with a handful of neighbors planting trees along one street in Maple Grove. A decade on, we're a volunteer-led network that works with residents, schools, and local businesses to make sustainable living something every community can take part in, not just talk about.

We keep things local and hands-on. No project is too small to matter, and no street is too far from the next one.
